ALETHRA™ PRIVACY POLICY

ALETHRA™ is a clarity technology platform developed by ALETHRA™ Inc. designed to prevent confusion and enhance consumer safety through AI-powered product identification and verification.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website, mobile applications, and related services ("Services").

By using ALETHRA™, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

A. Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and optional profile details when you create or manage an account.
  • User Inputs: Product scans, feedback, images, and comments submitted through the app.
  • Beta Testing Data: Voluntary performance and feedback data if you participate in the Beta program.

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, scan frequency, session duration, and crash logs.
  • Location Data (optional): Used only when you permit it, to improve accuracy of product recognition or locate nearby verified products.
  • Cookies and Analytics: Used to measure traffic, improve user experience, and diagnose performance issues.
3.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use collected information to:

  1. Operate, maintain, and improve ALETHRA™ Services.
  2. Train our AI systems to improve accuracy and safety outcomes.
  3. Respond to inquiries, bug reports, and support requests.
  4. Send you important updates, feature notices, or policy changes.
  5. Analyze anonymized usage trends for research and innovation.

"We never sell personal data to third parties."

4.

DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We may share information only in the following limited situations:

  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who help operate the platform (hosting, analytics, testing).
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect rights, safety, and integrity.
  • Corporate Events: In the case of a merger or acquisition, subject to this policy's protections.

All partners must comply with strict confidentiality and data protection standards.

5.

DATA
RETENTION

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

6.

DATA
SECURITY

ALETHRA™ employs encryption, secure servers, and multi-layered safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. While no system is entirely immune, we are committed to continuous monitoring and improvement of our security posture.

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@myalethra.com

8.

USER DATA DELETION REQUESTS

You have the right to request deletion of any personal data we hold about you.
